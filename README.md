# 🌍 Air Quality Dashboard (India 2022–2025)

## 📌 Problem Statement
Conduct Market Fit Research for Air Purifier Development using AQI Analytics.

The objective was to analyze air quality trends and identify:
- High pollution regions
- Key pollutants
- Seasonal patterns
- Demand triggers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides:
- Top 5 polluted & clean states
- Weekend vs weekday AQI comparison
- Monthly AQI trends
- EV adoption vs AQI correlation
- Dominant pollutants across southern states

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Delhi shows consistently high AQI levels
- Northeastern states have better air quality
- Pollution peaks during winter (Nov–Dec)
- Weak correlation between EV adoption and AQI
- PM2.5 & PM10 are dominant pollutants
